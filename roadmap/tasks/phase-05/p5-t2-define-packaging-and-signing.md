# P5-T2 - Define Packaging and Signing

## Phase

[Phase 05 - Reliability and Distribution](../../phases/phase-05-reliability-and-distribution.md)

## Objective

Establish how Knuthflow will be built, packaged, signed, and installed on target platforms.

## Deliverables

- Packaging targets per operating system
- Signing requirements and credential ownership
- Artifact naming and versioning rules
- Installer or distribution channel plan

## Dependencies

- [P1-T1](../phase-01/p1-t1-scaffold-electron-shell.md)

## Acceptance Criteria

- Distribution targets are explicit
- Signing steps are known and assignable
- Build outputs are consistent across releases
- Packaging work is no longer blocked by ambiguity

Not Implemented
