# P32-T3 - Role-Targeted Sections

## Phase

[Phase 32 - Collaboration and Review Handoff](../../phases/phase-32-collaboration-review-handoff.md)

## Objective

Generate developer, product, QA, and maintenance review sections inside review bundles.

## Deliverables

- Generate developer section with changed files, diffs, build/test evidence, and technical notes.
- Generate product section with brief, scope, acceptance criteria, screenshots, and delivery summary.
- Generate QA section with validation gates, visual evidence, known limitations, and test gaps.
- Generate maintenance section with run history, decisions, policy overrides, and follow-up risks.
- Let operators include or exclude role sections before export.

## Dependencies

- Bundle generation and source data are available.
- Visual evidence and validation artifacts are available where supported.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Review bundles include role-targeted summaries.
- Sections are concise and traceable to evidence.
- Missing evidence is called out instead of hidden.
- Operators can choose appropriate sections for the audience.
